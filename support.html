Common questions

What's free, and what needs the unlock? Entering parts by hand and viewing the optimized on-screen cut plan are free, forever. A single one-time purchase unlocks the pro tools: the cabinet, drawer, door/face and face-frame generators, exploded 2D & 3D views, shop-floor cutting mode, "Optimize harder," and PDF & shopping-list export and sharing.

How much is the unlock? $9.99 (US) — a one-time purchase, not a subscription. There are no recurring charges.

I bought it — how do I use it on another device? Tap "Restore purchase" on the unlock screen. Your purchase restores on any device signed in to the same Apple ID.

I paid but the app still shows locks. Make sure you're signed in to the App Store with the Apple ID you purchased with, then tap "Restore purchase" on the unlock screen. If it still doesn't unlock, email us and we'll help.

Can I get a refund? Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, and choose your Cutpath purchase.

Is the Face frame generator finished? The face-frame generator is currently labeled BETA. It works, but it has a lot of variables — double-check its cut list against your build before cutting. Feedback is very welcome.

Does Cutpath collect my data? No. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
