Cutpath — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Cutpath is a woodworking cut-list and cutting-layout app. It is built to run entirely on your device. This policy explains how Cutpath handles your information.

Information we collect

None. Cutpath does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share any personal information. We operate no servers, require no account or sign-in, and use no analytics.

Your data stays on your device

Everything you enter — parts, stock, saved projects, and app settings — is stored only on your device using the operating system's on-device storage. It is never sent to us, and we cannot see it.

Purchases

Cutpath offers a single one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full app. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Cutpath never receives or stores your payment details. Apple's handling of purchase information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

No tracking or advertising

Cutpath contains no advertising, no tracking technologies, and no third-party analytics or marketing SDKs.

Children's privacy

Cutpath does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
